## Frequently Asked Questions

**How does the city matching work?**
Relocate Muse asks you 11 questions about your budget, climate preferences,
walkability needs, school priorities, crime tolerance, and lifestyle. It then
scores and ranks cities based on how well they match your answers.

**Why am I not seeing any results?**
This usually happens when your filters are very strict. Try relaxing your
budget range or crime tolerance and run the quiz again.

**Can I search for cities outside the US?**
Yes! On the last quiz question, select Canada, Mexico, Caribbean & Central
America, South America, or "Surprise me anywhere" to include international
cities in your results.

**What is City Roulette?**
City Roulette is a fun mode that randomly picks a city for you. Tap the
Roulette tab at the bottom of the app, toggle on international cities if
you'd like, and hit Spin.

**Where do the real estate listings come from?**
Relocate Muse links directly to Zillow (for US cities) or Google (for
international cities) with your bedroom, bathroom, and budget filters
pre-applied. Relocate Muse does not host or control any listing data.

**Does the app store any of my data?**
No. Relocate Muse does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
All quiz answers stay on your device and are cleared when you start over.
See our full [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md).
